Our Programs

Three programs.
One whole child.

Every Bright Stays visit combines creative enrichment, academic support, and genuine companionship — tailored to each child's age, interests, and current needs.

Program 01
🎨

Creative Enrichment

Art, music, storytelling, and hands-on play delivered bedside by trained volunteers. Every session is age-appropriate, interest-guided, and designed to spark joy — regardless of mobility or energy levels.

  • Drawing, painting, and collage activities
  • Storytelling and imaginative play sessions
  • Simple instrument exploration and music listening
  • Holiday and seasonal themed projects
  • Take-home keepsakes and artwork
Program 02
📚

Academic Continuity

One-on-one tutoring, reading support, and homework help so children can keep up with their grade level and return to school without a gap in confidence or knowledge.

  • Personalized tutoring across core subjects
  • Coordination with the child's home school when possible
  • Reading aloud and literacy exercises
  • Grade-appropriate workbooks and activities
  • STEM exploration through puzzles and games
Program 03
🤝

Companionship & Connection

Consistent volunteer friendships that make long stays feel less lonely. The same volunteers return week after week — building trust, familiarity, and a genuine bond that children look forward to.

  • Recurring weekly visits from assigned volunteers
  • Board games, card games, and interactive play
  • Casual conversation and active listening
  • Support for siblings and family members present
  • Cultural and interest-based matching
The Visit

How a Bright Stays
visit actually works.

Every visit is structured but flexible — designed to meet children where they are, on the day that they need it most.

1

Volunteer Arrival & Check-In

Volunteers check in with the nursing station to confirm the child is available and note any updated care instructions or activity restrictions.

2

Personalized Engagement

Based on the child's interests and that day's energy, volunteers lead creative, academic, or social activities — or a blend of all three.

3

Family Time

Caregivers and siblings can be included, and parents get a rare moment of relief knowing their child is in good hands.

4

Visit Log & Follow-Up

Volunteers log activity notes, morale observations, and any flag for the care team — keeping hospital staff fully in the loop.

📋 Every Volunteer Is Trained

Before their first visit, all Bright Stays volunteers complete a formal onboarding covering child development, hospital etiquette, trauma-informed communication, and activity facilitation.

🏥 Fully Hospital-Coordinated

We work with hospital child life specialists and nursing teams to ensure every visit is safe, appropriate, and complementary to the child's medical care plan.

Eligibility

Who we serve.

Bright Stays prioritizes children most likely to benefit — those who have been hospitalized long enough that normal childhood is genuinely at risk.

⏱️

Long-Term Patients

Children with anticipated stays of 7+ days, including oncology, cardiology, and chronic condition patients.

📅

Ages 4–17

Programs are designed to scale with the child's developmental stage, from early learners to teenagers.

🌍

All Backgrounds

Multilingual volunteers and culturally sensitive programming ensure no child is left out due to language or background.

💛

Free, Always

Bright Stays is completely free for patients and families. Our funding comes from donors, grants, and partnerships.
