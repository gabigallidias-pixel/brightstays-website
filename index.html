What We Do

Three pillars.
One mission.

Every Bright Stays program is designed around what hospitalized children actually need — connection, learning, and joy.

🎨

Creative Enrichment

Art, music, storytelling, and play — delivered bedside by trained volunteers. Because creativity heals as much as medicine.

📚

Academic Continuity

Tutoring, reading, and schoolwork support so children don't fall behind and return home with their confidence intact.

🤝

Genuine Companionship

Consistent, friendly faces — not just one-time visitors. Relationships that make long stays feel less isolating, week after week.

The Gap

Most hospitals treat the body.
Few nurture the whole child.

Long-term pediatric patients face emotional isolation, developmental regression, and academic setbacks that medicine alone can't fix. Today, there's no organization providing consistent, recurring enrichment at the bedside — across all three dimensions at once.

Read the Full Problem →

The Bright Stays Difference

We don't show up once. We come back, week after week, building real relationships with the children and families who need us most.

✓ Recurring, scheduled visits — not one-time events
✓ Trained volunteers matched to each child's interests
✓ Creative + academic + emotional support in one program
✓ Hospital-embedded, fully coordinated with care teams
Our Reach

Starting in Boston.
Growing to New York.

Bright Stays is piloting at Boston Children's Hospital, with Summer 2025 expansion into NYU Langone, Columbia, and NewYork-Presbyterian.

New York City
